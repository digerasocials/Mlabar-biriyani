Our Story

The Heartbeat of the Night Riders

Malabar isn't just a restaurant; it's an emotion for our city. For years, we have been the ultimate destination for late-night cravings, family dinners, and foodies seeking the authentic taste of Mughlai and coastal cuisine.

From our signature Mutton Biryani cooked on dum with tender meat and fragrant spices, to our smoky charcoal kebabs and fiery specials, every dish is a testament to our legacy.
